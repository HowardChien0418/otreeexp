Task description

In this decision-making task, there are two buckets.
{% if player.is_player_s %} As a Player S, your job is to draw a ball from the chosen bucket.

If the ball you drew is Gold, you don’t need to do further action in this round.
{% else %} As a Player R, your job is to name the color of the bucket, Silver or Gold.
Before making your decision, you will observe Player S’s action.
Player S either reports you that he or she has drawn a Silver ball or remains silent.
