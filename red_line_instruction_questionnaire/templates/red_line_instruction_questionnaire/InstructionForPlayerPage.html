In this experiment each participant will be assigned to one of two roles: S or R.

Today you are a Player {{ player.role }}.

You will play this role for 50 rounds, and each round is a separate decision task.

Please read the instruction sheet carefully.

{% if player.role == "R" %} As a Player R, your job is to name the color of the bucket, Silver or Gold. Before making your decision, you will observe Player S’s action. Player S either reports you that he or she has drawn a Silver ball or remains silent. Your payoff in each round is determined as follows.

  1. If you choose Silver and the color of the bucket is Silver, you will receive a payoff that is randomly drawn from the interval [30 and 70] or [10 and 90] at the beginning of each round.
  2. You will know the exact value, but Player S only knows that the value is randomly drawn from the interval, and every value within the interval is equally likely to be drawn.
  3. If you choose Gold and the color of the bucket is Gold, you will receive a payoff of 50.
  4. If you choose the wrong color of the bucket, you will receive a payoff of 0.
  5. If you choose the color of the bucket correctly, Player S will receive a payoff of {{group.v}}.
  6. If you choose the wrong color of the bucket, Player S will receive a payoff of 0.
  7. If Player S reports Silver to you but you decide to choose Gold, {{group.x}} points will be deducted from his or her payoff.
{% else %} As a Player S, your job is to draw a ball from the chosen bucket and, if the ball you draw is Silver, decide whether to report Silver to Player S. If the ball you draw is Gold, you don’t need to do further action in this round. Your payoff in each round is determined jointly by your and Player R’s action.
  1. If Player R chooses the color of the bucket correctly, you will receive a payoff of {{group.v}}.
  2. If Player R chooses the wrong color of the bucket, you will receive a payoff of 0.
  3. If you report Silver but Player R chooses Gold, {{group.x}} points will be deducted from your payoff.
  4. If Player R chooses Gold and the color of the bucket is Gold, he or she will receive a payoff 50.
  5. If Player R chooses the wrong color of the bucket, he or she will receive a payoff 0.
  6. If Player R chooses Silver and the color of the bucket is Silver, he or she will receive a payoff that could be any number randomly drawn between [10 and 90] or [30 and 70] at the beginning of each round.
{% endif %}
